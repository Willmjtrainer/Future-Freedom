Future Freedom
For Families in Disability Systems, a system that holds when they cannot.

Overview
For families in disability systems who want to secure their child’s future while reclaiming their own. Most families worry about what happens when they are no longer there. Yet a disability label should not define a future. Parents can build futures filled with purpose, security, and joy for their children and for themselves.

Framework:

Decode the Fog.
Connect the Dots.
Plan the Future.
Empower Leaders.
Live the Infrastructure.

Proof of Impact:

Families no longer live in fear and chaos.
They have clarity and confidence.
They have clear strategies to build their future and believe that systems will hold when they can no longer do so.
